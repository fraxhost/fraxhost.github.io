Greetings!
I’m Ahmed Ryan, a dedicated Software Engineer with around 3 years of experience in software development, particularly in the web domain. I graduated with a Bachelor’s degree in Software Engineering from the Institute of Information Technology, University of Dhaka, recognized as a leading institution for Software Engineering in Bangladesh. My research interests include security test automation, malware analysis, and exploit development to enhance software security, inspired by my final year project and industry experience.
In 2020, I participated in a national CTF competition where my team achieved 20th place out of 234, which sparked my interest in software security. In addition, I completed an internship in my 7th semester, focusing on an in-house HR portal where I emphasized secure software development by applying OWASP standards to protect APIs from external threats and identify and mitigate potential vulnerabilities. These foundational experiences led to my final semester project, where I implemented a tool based on a research paper for automating web API testing. Currently, I develop secure web-based software solutions for European clients and continue to expand my security knowledge through several certifications.
Furthermore, I am researching how to apply in-context learning capabilities of AI models to improve the automated detection of community smells in software under Intelligent Systems and HCI Research Group, further enhancing my research skills. This combination of industry and research experience has equipped me to effectively bridge theory and practice, and I am now actively seeking a PhD position in Software Security for Fall ‘25.
Research Interest Areas:
  • Security Test Automation
  • Malware Analysis and Detection Techniques
  • Vulnerability Research and Exploit Development
