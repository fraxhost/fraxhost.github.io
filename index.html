Greetings!
I’m Ahmed Ryan, a Software Engineer with around 3 years of experience in software development, particularly in the web domain. I graduated with a Bachelor’s in Software Engineering from the Institute of Information Technology, University of Dhaka, recognized as a leading institution in Bangladesh. My interest in software security began in 2020 when I participated in a national CTF competition. During my internship in 2022 semester, I worked on an in-house HR portal, applying OWASP standards to secure APIs from external threats and mitigate vulnerabilities.
In my final year, I developed a tool for automating web API testing, based on a research paper. Currently, I work at Cefalo, a reputable software company, where I create secure web-based software solutions for European clients. I continue expanding my security knowledge through certifications. Additionally, I’m involved in research with the Intelligent Systems and HCI Research Group, where I’m exploring how to apply AI to improve the automated detection of community smells in software systems. This blend of industry experience and research has equipped me to effectively bridge theory and practice, and I’m actively seeking a PhD position in Software Security for Fall ’25.
Research Interest Areas:
  • Security Test Automation
  • Malware Analysis and Detection Techniques
  • Vulnerability Research and Exploit Development
