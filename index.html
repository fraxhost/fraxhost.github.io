Hi, I'm Ahmed Ryan.

I'm a PhD student in Computer Science at the University of Alabama, researching software supply chain security and machine learning as a Graduate Research Assistant to Dr. Rayhanur Rahman.

Email GitHub LinkedIn

Previously I worked at Cefalo, building secure web-based software solutions for European clients. My interest in security started in 2020 when I competed in a national CTF competition (Top 20 of 234 teams). During a 2022 internship I applied OWASP standards to develop secure web applications, and later did research with the Intelligent Systems and HCI Research Group on AI-driven community smell detection.

Right now I work with malicious packages and machine learning to defend software supply chains against modern threats. This blend of industry, research, and security training lets me bridge theory and practice.
